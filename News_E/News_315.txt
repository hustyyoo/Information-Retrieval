Readout of President Donald J. Trump’s Call with President Abdel Fattah Al Sisi of Egypt
				Statements & Releases			

			Foreign Policy		

Issued on:
March 4, 2018
President Donald J. Trump spoke today with President Abdel Fattah Al Sisi of Egypt.  President Trump and President Al Sisi discussed opportunities to enhance the American-Egyptian partnership on a range of security and economic issues.  The leaders discussed Russia and Iran’s irresponsible support of the Assad regime’s brutal attacks against innocent civilians.  President Trump and President Al Sisi agreed to work together on ending the humanitarian crisis in Syria and achieving Arab unity and security in the region.
