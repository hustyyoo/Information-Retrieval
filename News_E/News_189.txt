President Donald J. Trump Signs H.R. 1177 into Law
				Statements & Releases			

			Economy & Jobs		

Issued on:
March 26, 2018
On Monday, March 26, 2018, the President signed into law:H.R. 1177, the “Removing Outdated Restrictions to Allow for Job Growth,” which directs the Secretary of Agriculture to release the deed restriction pertaining to certain land previously conveyed to the City of Old Town, Maine.
