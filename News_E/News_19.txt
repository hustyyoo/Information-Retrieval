Readout of President Donald J. Trump’s Call with President Sauli Niinistö of Finland
				Statements & Releases			

			Foreign Policy		

Issued on:
April 23, 2018
President Donald J. Trump spoke today with President Sauli Niinistö of Finland.  The leaders discussed Finland’s plans for its remaining time as Chair of the Arctic Council.  Both leaders affirmed that it is essential to increase security in the Arctic.
