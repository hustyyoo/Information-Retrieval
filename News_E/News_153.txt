President Donald J. Trump's Support for Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania
				Fact Sheets			

			Foreign Policy		

Issued on:
April 3, 2018
We are very protective of the Baltic region… We have great friends there.
								President Donald J. Trump							BOOSTING DEFENSE AND SECURITY COOPERATION: President Donald J. Trump supports strengthening efforts to enhance the defense and security of our Baltic NATO Allies.ADVANCING ENERGY SECURITY: President Trump reinforces Baltic efforts to diversify energy sources and increase competitiveness in energy markets.ENRICHING PEOPLE-TO-PEOPLE TIES: President Trump advances closer relations between the American, Estonian, Latvian, and Lithuanian populations through exchanges and cultural programs.
