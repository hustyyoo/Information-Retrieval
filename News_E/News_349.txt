President Trump Announces the Head of Delegation for the Presidential Delegation to the Republic of Korea to Attend the Opening Ceremony of the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games in PyeongChang, Gangwon Province, Republic of Korea
				Statements & Releases			
Issued on:
February 27, 2018
President Donald J. Trump today announced the designation of a Presidential Delegation to the Republic of Korea to attend the Opening Ceremony and events of the 2018 PyeongChang Paralympic Winter Games beginning Friday, March 9, 2018.The Honorable Kirstjen M. Nielsen, Secretary of Homeland Security, will lead the delegation.Members of the Presidential Delegation will be announced in the coming days.
