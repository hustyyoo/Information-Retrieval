President Donald J. Trump Signs H.R. 984 and H.R. 4641 into Law
				Statements & Releases			
Issued on:
January 29, 2018
On Monday, January 29, 2018, the President signed into law:H.R. 984, the “Thomasina E. Jordan Indian Tribes of Virginia Federal Recognition Act of 2017,” which provides Federal recognition to six Indian Tribes in the State of Virginia; andH.R. 4641, which authorizes the President to award the Medal of Honor to Gunnery Sergeant John L. Canley for acts of valor during the Vietnam War while serving in the Marine Corps.
